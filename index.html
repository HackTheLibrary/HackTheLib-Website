    FAQ

    Who Can Sign Up?

    Anyone can sign up! No experience is necessary, even to compete! We will have workshops for newcomers to learn as well as advanced programmers to hone their skills and help in the competition.

    How many days it the event?

    The Hackathon is 3 days long from June 24th-June 26th

    What Does it Cost?

    The event is completely free to participate in.

    Can I Win Prizes?

    Of course you can! There will be prizes for the top projects in each category in form of a gift card!.

    What Can I Make?

    Anything you want, from games to websites to the next big social media platform. HackSTL is a time to experiment, get creative, and build something amazing.

    How do I get HackTheLib SWAG?

    Due to current circumstances, HackTheLib SWAG (t-shirts, backpacks) will only be available for local pick up from the Schaumburg Library.
